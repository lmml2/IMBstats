How to get started We quote all work on a per project basis so you know the exact amount you will pay for the whole project, no matter how long it will take to complete the initial work or how many questions or requests you may ask later on. I also offer free initial consultation to determine the exact requirements and best route forward before starting the contract. In order for me to provide you with an upfront quote of the total cost, please send me all material and detailed instructions of what you need help with: For data analysis help, please send me: #1.The data and instrument – (if you have used a questionnaire) #2. Study proposal or Introduction and Methodology Chapters If you do not have a written proposal, please send me: a).The data and instrument – (if you have used a questionnaire) b).List of your study objectives. c).Brief background information about your research. For help with research proposals or prospectus, concept papers, IRB applications:Please send me the most up-to-date draft of the report and highlight/mention the areas you need help with. If you have received comments from your committee or methods advisor please send me the comments. Without this information, I will not be capable of providing you with upfront quote of the cost. I will need to see the material that you need help with because each research or project is different in size, type and complexity. Please make sure to let me know exactly what it is you need help with. I also need to know if you have a deadline. Once I receive your research material and instruction, I will study them and get back to you with my FREE upfront exact cost along with a turn-around to complete the work and proposed plan of action for your review and approval. I prefer to communicate via-email. I must review and study the project material to determine the cost. You will receive an accurate quote by sending me the data, research proposal or the objectives, and a brief background about your research. The information you provide must be complete and precise. It is not possible to provide quotes over the phone or without the required information.
